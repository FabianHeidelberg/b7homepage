Registration

We are fans of tradition so the registration date will be as always the 1st 2016. We have a special email-address for registration:
register@b7-tournament.de
Please write your full name into the email as well as your NAF number and if you already know the team you will leet to glory at the tournament.
The fee will be 18 Euro for NAF Members and for those who aren't members yet you can sign up or renew your membership at the tournament. Please mention in the email if you aren't a NAF member at the time.

In 2016 we are able to offer 32 places for the B7. Because of that we will play five rounds at the tournament and you can sleep a little longer on Sunday ;) Registration follows the timestamp of your email.
See you at B7 in 2016!
