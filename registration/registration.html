Registration

We are fans of tradition so the registration date will be as always the 1st 2018. We have a special email-address for registration:
register@b7-tournament.de
Please write your full name into the email as well as your NAF number and if you already know the team you will lead to glory at the tournament.
The fee will be 18 Euro for NAF Members and for those who aren't members yet you can sign up or renew your membership at the tournament. Please mention in the email if you aren't a NAF member at the time.
