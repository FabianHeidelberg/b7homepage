Registration

We are fans of tradition so the registration date will be as always the 1st 2019. We have a special email-address for registration:
register@b7-tournament.de
The tournament will be at the Auryn Con from 06.07.2019 - 07.07.2019! Please write your full name into the email as well as your NAF number and if you already know the team you will lead to glory at the tournament.
The fee will be 18 Euro for NAF Members and for those who aren't members yet you can sign up or renew your membership at the tournament. Please mention in the email if you aren't a NAF member at the time.

Be aware of that with the registration you are agreeing to that your firstname and part of your lastname will be displayed one the webpage. We will erase the data shortly after the tournament and we will only store the resultsheet of score. If you are not agree to these terms please let us know and we will mask your name.
