🏈 BLOOD, GLORY & A RETURN TO THE PITCH – B7 IS BACK! 🏈

The crowd roars, the turf trembles, and somewhere in the distance a goblin referee is already making questionable calls…

We are proud (and slightly concerned for everyone’s safety) to announce: B7 is BACK!

Once again, the clash of armor, bone, and questionable tactics will take place at the legendary Auryn Con in Böblingen. The sacred grounds will echo with the sound of crunching tackles and triumphant war cries as coaches from near and far gather to prove their worth.

But this year… things are a little different.

⚡ A Twist in the Game

For the very first time, B7 will be played in Sevens format!

Faster, bloodier, and even more unpredictable — exactly how Nuffle likes it. Expect brutal drives, desperate plays, and absolutely no time to catch your breath.

👥 Coaches, Old and New

We are thrilled to welcome back familiar faces — the veterans, the legends, the rule-benders — and equally excited to see new challengers step onto the pitch. Fresh blood is always appreciated… literally.
